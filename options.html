To change the hotkeys, click on the textbox and press your desired hotkey combination. To enable/disable a hotkey, click on any button on the right.

Warning: Some shortcut combinations may not work properly. The defaults are recommended. Also, it is best to restart the browser after changing the options.

Note: hotkeys are disabled while on input fields.
